Embed Grumpy Emoji on your Site

This is an easy one: Add these lines to your HTML file, and nasty, distracting emoji, that managed to slip your attention, will instantly be taken care of.

<link rel="stylesheet" href="grumpy.css">
<style>
  body { font-family: Grumpy, sans-serif; }
</style>
